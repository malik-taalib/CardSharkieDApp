## Platform 1: Base Builder Grants (Primary Target)

### What It Is

Base's own grant program, run by the Base team at Coinbase. This is your highest-priority target since Card Sharkie is built specifically for Base.

### How It Works

**This is not a traditional application process.** Base primarily discovers grantees through their own scouting on Twitter, Farcaster, and community nominations. You can self-nominate, but the real key is **visibility** — being active in the Base community and showing shipped work.

### What They Look For (Directly From Their Documentation)

- **Is the builder creating something unique and fun?** ✅ P2P skill-based card gaming with trustless escrow is both unique and engaging
- **Is the builder bringing more users onchain?** ✅ Card Sharkie converts casual card players (crypto-naive) into Base wallet holders
- **Is the builder's contribution live and making an impact?** This is where you need to ship to testnet ASAP

### Grant Size

1–5 ETH (currently). Our application targets 3–5 ETH.

### KYC/Compliance

If selected, Base collects **W8/W9 forms** from all grant recipients for tax, compliance, and legal purposes. As a US-based LLC (Vivente Unlimited), you'd submit a W-9.

### Step-by-Step Setup

**Step 1: Get on Builder Score (builderscore.xyz)**

This is Base's weekly rewards program — 2 ETH distributed weekly to active builders. It's your entry point.

- Go to https://www.builderscore.xyz/
- Connect your Coinbase Wallet (or MetaMask configured for Base)
- Link your GitHub account
- Link your Twitter/X account
- Link your Farcaster account (create one if you don't have one — see below)
- Deploy Card Sharkie contract to Base Sepolia testnet (this boosts your score significantly)
- Your "Builder Score" increases as you ship code and engage with the ecosystem

**Step 2: Create a Farcaster Account**

Farcaster is the decentralized social network where the Base community is most active. The Base grants team actively scouts here.

- Go to https://warpcast.com (the main Farcaster client)
- Sign up (requires a small onchain registration fee, ~$5)
- Fill out your profile: name, bio mentioning "Building Card Sharkie on @base", link to your GitHub
- Follow @base, @buildonbase, and @jessepollak (Base lead)
- Start posting your build progress in the /base and /base-builds channels

**Step 3: Self-Nominate**

- Go to the nomination form: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfXuEzmiAzRhie_z9raFCF1BXweXgVt18o-DvBuRRgyTygL2A/viewform
- Fill it out with your project details, link to deployed testnet contract, and your dApp demo URL
- Note: they won't respond unless you're selected, so don't wait on this — keep building and posting

**Step 4: Build in Public**

The Base grants team finds builders through Twitter, Farcaster, and community nominations. Your build-in-public content strategy:

- **Weekly thread on Twitter/X:** "Building Card Sharkie on @base — Week [X] update" with screenshots, contract addresses, metrics
- **Farcaster posts:** Shorter, more frequent updates in /base-builds channel
- **Tag @BuildOnBase** in your posts (but don't spam — quality updates only)
- **Engage with other Base builders** — comment on their work, share insights

### Base Batches (Longer-Term Play)

Once Card Sharkie is live on mainnet with real users, you can apply for **Base Batches** — a comprehensive founder program with:

- Direct mentorship from Base team members
- Access to Coinbase Ventures and crypto VC introductions
- Structured incubation (4-week program)
- Pitch day to investors

This is the bigger play and would be appropriate once you have mainnet traction (3-6 months out). URL: https://basebatches.xyz

## Platform 2: AlphaGrowth (Multi-Grant Pipeline)

### What It Is

AlphaGrowth is a platform that matches crypto projects to grant programs and investors. They track 2,000+ grant programs representing over $10 billion in available funding. Their "Grants App" lets you create one profile and apply to multiple programs.

### How It Works

Unlike Base (which is discovery-based), AlphaGrowth is application-based. You create a project profile, and the platform helps you identify and apply to matching grant programs across chains.

### Step-by-Step Setup

**Step 1: Create Your Profile**

- Go to https://alphagrowth.io/grants/apply
- Click "Create Profile" or "Connect" (you can connect with a wallet or create a standard account)
- Fill out your project profile with the following information:

**Project Name:** Card Sharkie

**One-Liner:** Trustless P2P skill-based card gaming with on-chain escrow on Base

**Description (use this):**
Card Sharkie is a peer-to-peer skill-based card gaming platform built on Base (Coinbase L2) that uses Solidity smart contracts for trustless wager escrow. Players deposit ETH before each game, and winnings are automatically distributed when the game concludes. The platform supports Spades, Tonk, and Blackjack, with sub-cent transaction costs and 2-second finality. Built by Vivente Unlimited, a multi-product technology company with an 8-product portfolio spanning AI, gaming, messaging, and publishing.

**Category:** Gaming / DeFi / Consumer App

**Chain:** Base (also select Ethereum/EVM-compatible)

**Stage:** Prototype / Pre-Launch

**Team Size:** 1 (solo founder + AI development agents)

**Funding Sought:** $5,000–$15,000 (equivalent to 3-5 ETH)

**Website:** viventeunlimited.com

**Links:** GitHub repo, deployed testnet contract URL, dApp demo URL

**Step 2: Browse Matching Grants**

Once your profile is set up, AlphaGrowth will surface matching grant programs. Filter by:

- **Chain:** Base, Ethereum, Optimism (Base is part of the OP Stack)
- **Category:** Gaming, DeFi, Consumer
- **Grant Size:** $1K–$50K range
- **Type:** Development grants

**Step 3: Apply to Multiple Programs**

The key grants to target through AlphaGrowth beyond Base:

| Grant Program | Amount | Why It Fits |
|---|---|---|
| Base Builder Grants | 1-5 ETH | Primary target, built specifically for Base |
| Optimism RetroPGF | Varies | Base is on the OP Stack; open-source escrow contract qualifies |
| Ethereum Foundation ESP | $10K-$250K | If you open-source the escrow pattern as a public good |
| Coinbase CDP Grants | $3K-$10K | Integrating Coinbase Wallet/onramp |
| Arbitrum Gaming Ventures | Varies | Multi-chain expansion opportunity |

**Step 4: Track Applications**

AlphaGrowth provides a dashboard to track your application status across all programs. Check weekly and respond promptly to any requests for additional information.

## Platform 3: Grantfarm by Blockworks (Discovery & Research)

### What It Is

Grantfarm is a free grant aggregator — the largest real-time directory of crypto grants, RFPs, and bug bounties. Use it primarily for **research and discovery** rather than direct applications (most grants link out to their own application portals).

### How to Use It

**Step 1: Go to https://www.blockworks.com/grants**

**Step 2: Search with these filters:**

- **Grant Type:** Development
- **Ecosystem:** Base, Ethereum, Optimism
- **Project Category:** Gaming, AI, DeFi

**Step 3: Bookmark relevant programs and check back monthly** — new grants are added regularly.

**Step 4: For each interesting program, click through to the program's own site and apply directly.** Grantfarm is a discovery tool, not an application platform.

### Pro Tips for Grantfarm

- Search broadly — don't just look at "Gaming." Card Sharkie touches DeFi (escrow), consumer apps, and even AI (if you pitch the AI-assisted development angle)
- Look at the RFP (Request for Proposal) section — sometimes chains post specific problems they want solved, and a trustless gaming escrow might match
- Check bounties too — some protocols offer bounties for integrating their technology, which you could incorporate into Card Sharkie

## Grant Application Strategy: What Makes You Competitive

### Your Strengths (Lean Into These)

1. **Shipped code, not a pitch deck.** You have a working smart contract, a functional dApp prototype, and a comprehensive architecture spec. Most grant applicants have slide decks.

2. **Clear user onboarding story.** Card Sharkie has a concrete path from crypto-naive card player to Base wallet holder. Grant reviewers love measurable user acquisition strategies.

3. **Existing product portfolio.** Vivente Unlimited's 8-product lineup shows you're a serious builder who ships, not a one-project hopeful.

4. **Solo builder with AI workflow.** This is genuinely novel and interesting to the Base community. The fact that you built a production-ready smart contract, dApp, and architecture spec as a solo founder using AI collaboration is a compelling story.

5. **Multi-product Base expansion.** Card Sharkie isn't the end — it's the beginning of bringing an entire portfolio onchain. Goliath Prep credentials, DirectPlay royalties, CallSign identity — each one is more users and transactions for Base.

### Your Weaknesses (Address Proactively)

1. **No mainnet deployment yet.** Solution: Deploy to testnet immediately and include the Basescan link in all applications.

2. **Solo team.** Solution: Frame this as a strength ("lean, AI-augmented development") not a weakness. Many successful Base grants have gone to solo builders.

3. **New to blockchain development.** Solution: Your C#/.NET expertise is deep and transferable. The architecture spec demonstrates you understand the full stack including Nethereum integration.

4. **Regulatory uncertainty around skill gaming.** Solution: Acknowledge it briefly in applications but don't dwell on it. Frame it as "we're building the trustless infrastructure; jurisdictional compliance is built into the roadmap."
